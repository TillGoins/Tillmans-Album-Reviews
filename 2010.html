2010's Albums

Album: Obsessed Artist: Dan + Shay Favorite Song: How Not To Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: lowkey my least favorite yet… I liked lipstick and how not to and maybe one other song but the rest were kinda eh imo.

Album: stand-alone Artist: Morgan Wallen Favorite Song: Going Down Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Iconic for having spin you around but lowkey may be some of his best work. I will stand by those who claim 30+ songs is too much and shows you don’t know how to put together an album bc I’ve listened to the last two fully through and rarely listen to the fully after that first listen. Very fun listen!

Album: Dan + Shay Artist: Dan + Shay Favorite Song: All To Myself Rating: 8.5/10 Reasoning: If there is ever an Eagles country tribute album, I want these guys to cover one of these nights or Seven Bridges Road. I still think I enjoyed their first album better, but this album has their most iconic stuff. Very good harmonies, make a two nickels joke about them saying worst way (Which my brain then went to Riley green lol).

Album: Where It All Began Artist: Dan + Shay Favorite Song: Show You Off Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Solid suggestion again!! Enjoyed the upbeat feel of this album and the Myrtle call out!

Album: Badlands Artist: Halsey Favorite Song: New Americana Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Halsey has the best catalog of anybody who’s a major alt pop star rn that was active in the 2010’s. This album justifies that.
