1980's Albums

Album: Under Lock and Key Artist: Dokken Favorite Song: In my dreams Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: Pure 80’s Hair Metal

Album: Green Artist: R.E.M. Favorite Song: Stand Rating: 6/10 Reasoning: This album was better than the first album I listened to by them. I personally didn’t like some of these songs, but it wasn’t the worst album ever.

Album: The Joshua Tree Artist: U2 Favorite Song: Where the Streets Have No Name Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: The only thing holding them back from a perfect score is the final two songs

Album: Open Up and Say…Ahh!!! Artist: Poison Favorite Song: Every Rose has its thorn Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Every song was good, but there were a few that were different than what I expected.

Album: Hysteria Artist: Def Leppard Favorite Song: Pour some sugar on me Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: Anything by Def Leppard gets a 10.

Album: Cycles Artist: The Doobie Brothers Favorite Song: Need a little taste of your love Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: Yacht Rock.

Album: Time, Love, And Tenderness Artist: Micheal Bolton Favorite Song: Missing you now Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Really great writing and some really good love songs on here.

Album: Tell it to My Heart Artist: Taylor Dayne Favorite Song: Tell it to my heart Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: Good songs, very 80’s, not much to remember it by.

Album: Full Moon Fever Artist: Tom Petty (Aka Lucky) Favorite Song: I won’t back Down Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: all songs were good but the point taken away is bc there end didn’t hold up too well. Also all I hear is lucky from KOTH.

Album: Money For Nothing Artist: Dire Straits Favorite Song: Sultans of Swing Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: I am now a fan of dire straits, the guitar work is impressive.

Album: Thriller Artist: MJ THE GOAT Favorite Song: Beat It Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: THE BEST SELLING ALBUM OF ALL TIME. I loved this album, from getting excited about the Paul McCartney track that I had never heard to jamming to the legendary EVH guitar solo on beat it. I watched a video on him recently and it was really good.

Album: Third Stage Artist: Boston Favorite Song: Amanda Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: Boston albums are short but they contain a lot of great music. I enjoy them for that.

Album: Permanent Vacation Artist: Aerosmith Favorite Song: Ragdoll Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: St. John was also a good one.

Album: Journeyman Artist: Eric Clapton Favorite Song: Breaking Point Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: This man is underrated and I'm looking forward to hearing his greatest hits.

Album: Hits! Artist: Boz Scaggs Favorite Song: JoJo Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: Boz Scaggs gives off fruitcake vibes ngl but Fruity people make good music.

Album: Greatest Hits Artist: Whitesnake Favorite Song: Crying in the rain Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: I have come to love Whitesnake a lot bc of three of four CD’s of theirs. Crying in the rain is so fun to blast.

Album: Worlds Away Artist: Pablo Cruise Favorite Song: Sailing to Paradise Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: Tropical Vibes and Yacht Rock easily earn this a 10.

Album: Controversy Artist: Prince Favorite Song: Annie Christian Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: This album felt like it was still relevant to today’s world. The songs were all vibey and had me grooving so that’s why it deserves a 9.

Album: Yes I am Artist: Melissa Etheridge Favorite Song: Ruins Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: not as good as her self titled but very solid.

Album: Don’t Close Your Eyes Artist: Keith Whitley Favorite Song: When you say nothing at all Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: So Morgan Wallen is a major fan of this dude and even wrote a song named after him, I kinda see it but this album really only had two standouts.

Album: Vivid Artist: Living Color Favorite Song: What your favorite color? Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: This band is really cool, they switch genres on almost every song which reminds me of sleep token.

Album: Glass Houses Artist: Billy Joel Favorite Song: Don’t ask me why Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: I don’t really like piano man or any of his big hits but this was good.

Album: Skynrd’s Innrds Artist: Lynrd Skynrd Favorite Song: Workin’ for MCA Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: You know I love country and rock. You also know I love southern rock and this was a sweet tooth for me. Really enjoyed this album and also SWEET HOME ALABAMA!!!

Album: Classics Volume 15 Artist: STYX Favorite Song: Mr. Roboto Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Since this is another album I’m giving a second chance to I’m gonna be honest the first time around not many songs impressed me. This time around a lot did and I like the synth sound to each track. This band was really good and is definitely underrated.

Album: Get Lucky Artist: Loverboy Favorite Song: Lucky Ones Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Short, but still good.

Album: Look What The Cat Dragged In Artist: Poison Favorite song: #1 Bad Boy Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: This was better than Open Up and Say… AHHH. Enjoyed the tracks better.

Album: Greatest Hits - - Rock ‘n’ soul, Part 1 Artist: John Oates & Daryl Hall Favorite Song: Private Eyes Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: You don’t know how fun this was, I probably shouldn’t have been that hyper driving seven mile but lowkey this album was great.

Album: Time Pieces Artist: Eric Clapton Favorite Song: Layla Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: Journeyman was better

Album: Pickin’ on Nashville Artist: The Kentucky Headhunters Favorite Song: Dumas Walker Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Won’t give this album a 10 bc I had expectations and they weren't met and also I try to be wise about what I give a 10.

Album: Control Artist: Janet Jackson Favorite Song: Nasty Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: I was going to give this lower but it has grown on me majorly this week.

Album: Whitesnake Artist: Whitesnake Favorite Song: Crying in the rain Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: In a sense of giving this a 10, it does not compare to Honeymoon suite or any of my other 10’s but was still very good.

Album: Don’t Be Cruel Artist: Bobby Brown Favorite Song: My Prerogative Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Solid Album from a now controversial artist. The opening 4 tracks carry.

Album: Killin’ Time Artist: Clint Black Favorite Song: Straight from the factory Rating: 10/10 Reasoning: This may be one of the best prime country albums I’ve get listened to.

Album: Greatest Hits Artist: Journey Favorite Song: Separate Ways Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: I didn’t get to listen to much of this so it may need a re listen but it’s journey they’re on the Mount Rushmore of 80’s music

Album: The Sound Of Money Artist: Eddie Money Favorite Song: Take me Home Tonight Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Probably some of the best guitar playing I’ve heard from the CD’s

Album: Greatest Hits Artist: Nights Ranger Favorite Song: Don’t tell me you love me Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: Night Ranger is super overlooked but might be one of the best hair bands of the 80’s.

Album: Slide it in Artist: Whitesnake Favorite Song: Love ain’t no stranger Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Whitesnake’s best album. Maybe a hot take

Album: Classics Volume 12 Artist: Peter Frampton Favorite Song: All I want to be Rating: 9/10 Reasoning: Amazing riffs and amazing lyrics, totally see why he go inducted into the R&R HOF
