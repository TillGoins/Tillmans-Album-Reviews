2020's Albums

Album: Smokestack Town Artist: Ole 60 Genre(s): Country, Red Clay, Country Rock Favorite Song: Really Wanna Know Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: I’m giving this album a 7/10 due to the fact it’s lacking what makes ole 60 great, real, raw and relatable music. Their ep’s the pre date the album are some of the most emotional pieces of music I’ve ever heard and what draw me to like them. This album was about their hometown and I definitely feel like it’s stuck to that story with songs like Nancy avenue and Smokestack Town. Overall they keep it short and simple and don’t experiment much outside their comfort zone which is appreciated bc their steel guitar sound mixed with acoustics and punchy drums makes for some fun country music.

Album: Twisters: The Album Artist: Various Favorite Song: Already had it Rating: 7/10 Reasoning: Overall I knew this soundtrack was going to be stacked with the amount of country artists that I knew on it. The production on each song is great and the themed lyrics really add to this soundtrack especially on tracks like chasing the wind. I think tracks like country classic, and Caddo Country were unnecessary. This album gets a 7 due to it’s not a genre bending mind blowing soundtrack but rather a compilation of modern day country artists showcasing their writing abilities and singing capabilities.

Album: The Select Artist: Ty Myers Favorite Song: Never get tired Rating: 8/10 Reasoning: 17 Year Old Ty Myers makes his debut into the realm of country music (which is having a major peak in pop culture currently) with “The Select”. Starting the album off strong with ends of the earth a love song is a good call as it gets the listener pulled in immediately. Throughout the album it’s evident that Ty takes influences from other genres with tracks like ‘real world now’ blues guitar riff backed. One thing Ty does well is display his ability to show love with multiple love songs written. Ty also shows off his vocal ability throughout the album. Overall Ty Myers is going to make a name for himself if he keeps up the talent on this album.
